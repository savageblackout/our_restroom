Our Restroom is a movement to educate business owners about the benefits of providing gender non-specific single-stall restrooms. Patrons can anonymously submit the name and address of a business that has single-stall restrooms that are gendered, and we will do the rest! The business will receive a postcard with facts about the benefits of creating a space for all of their patrons that is comfortable and easy to use.

Simply click on the "Educate A Business!" link, fill out the required form with the business name and address. Check out the "Show Businesses" page to see a listing of all the businesses that have been added to the database. Search the database using the business name. If you see that the business you would like to add is already in the database, simply click on the "up vote" plus ("+") sign next the business!

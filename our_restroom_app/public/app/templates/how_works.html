How It Works

Simply click on the "Educate A Business!" link, fill out the required form with the business name and address. Check out the "Show Businesses" page to see a listing of all the businesses that have been added to the database. Search the database using the business name. If you see that the business you would like to add is already in the database, simply click on the "up vote" plus ("+") sign next the business!

OURrestroom is a project conceived by Kristin Russo. After feeling frustrated by the many businesses she patroned that gendered their single-stall restrooms, she decided to do something about it.
Speaking with some small business owners, she found that lack of education about the benefits of gender-non-specific, single-stall restrooms was often the reasoning behind many business restroom policies. These discussions helped inform how OURrestroom would be built.
Providing anonymity to users and contacting business owners in a non- confrontational manner are integral features of the OURrestroom project. Business patrons want to feel as though they are being heard, but may not feel comfortable confronting business owners face-to-face. OURrestroom offers a space for users to be a part of a movement without the risks involved in in-person confrontation.
